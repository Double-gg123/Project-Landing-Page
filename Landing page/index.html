Different breeds of fluffy dogs

The American Eskimo dog is truly a puffball of fur. It is nice and friendly to play with.

One look at the curls on a Barbet is enough to make you fall head over tail for this fluffball!

These tiny goofballs are like small snowmen ready to frolic wherever the two of you may go

The German Spitz has a double coat and one of the happiest faces in the animal kingdom

One of the fluffiest dog breeds is the Samoyed, which is known for its thick, white coat that was originally bred to help the Samoyed people of Siberia herd reindeer and protect them from wolves. The breed's coat is so thick and fluffy that they actually have a built-in air conditioning system to help keep them cool in hot weather. The dense undercoat of the Samoyed reflects sunlight and keeps them warm in cold weather, while the outer coat insulates and protects them from the elements.
